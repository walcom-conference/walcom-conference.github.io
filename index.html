About WALCOM Conference

To provide an international forum for researchers working in the areas of algorithms and computation, the first Workshop on Algorithms and Computation (WALCOM 2007) was held on February 12, 2007 in Dhaka, Bangladesh and was organized by Bangladesh Academy of Sciences. WALCOM 2008, WALCOM 2010, WALCOM 2012, WALCOM 2015 and WALCOM 2018 were held in Bangladesh; whereas WALCOM 2009, WALCOM 2011, WALCOM 2013, WALCOM 2014 and WALCOM 2019 were held in India. WALCOM 2016 was held in Nepal. WALCOM 2017 and WALCOM 2023 were held in Taiwan, WALCOM 2020 was held in Singapore. The name of this conference has been changed as Conference and Workshops on Algorithms and Computation since WALCOM 2017. WALCOM 2021 was held fully online and was conducted by Myanmar and Japan. WALCOM 2022 was held in Indonesia in Hybrid mode. The 18th International Conference and Workshops on Algorithms and Computation (WALCOM 2024) will be held in Kanazawa, Japan during March 18- March 20, 2024.The 19th International Conference and Workshops on Algorithms and Computation (WALCOM 2025) was held in Chengdu, China during February 28 to March 02, 2025.The 20th International Conference and Workshops on Algorithms and Computation (WALCOM 2026) will be held in Perugia, Italy, from March 4 to 6, 2026.
